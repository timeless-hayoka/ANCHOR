How this differs from scanners and LLM claims

Scanner output

Useful for signals, weak for conclusions. A detector hit may identify a suspicious pattern without proving the path, the impact, or the feasibility.

LLM analysis

Useful for hypothesis framing, weak for authority. An LLM can explain a mechanism cleanly and still be wrong about whether the exploit actually works.

ANCHOR proof gate

Signals and reasoning feed the workflow, but only reproduction and review promote a claim. That is the difference between a plausible story and a finding.
